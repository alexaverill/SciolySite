Scioly.org Staff

Administrators

Site administrators (admins) are at the head of the staffing hierarchy. They take care of many of the behind-the-scenes functions of scioly.org, including dealing with team forum creation, site upkeep, and moderation. If you would like to request a team forum, or discover a problem with the site (such as permissions within a forum), the admins are the people you should talk to.

Gallery Administrators

A gallery administrator functions similarly to a site administrator, but focuses their attention to the image gallery. They maintain the functionality and content of the image gallery. Any questions regarding the gallery should be directed to the gallery administrator.

Moderators

Global moderators oversee the day-to-day activities of the forums. They monitor the forums for spam and posts that break the rules, and determine if users have crossed the line. If you see a spam post (one with no legitimate purpose, or solely for advertising), or have a problem with the content of a post beyond a simple disagreement, you should report it to a global moderator.

Wikimods

Wiki moderators are staff members assigned to work solely with the wiki. This involves inspecting pages and edits to ensure quality control, as well as continuing to work themselves to improve the wiki. Vandalism, though not typically prevalent on the scioly.org wiki, should be reported to the wiki moderators, and any questions regarding the wiki should also be sent their way.
